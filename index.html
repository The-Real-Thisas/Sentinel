Sentinel

An OS where your privacy and security is our top priority.

Its open-source and developed by Thisas 

No more should we allow cooperations to harvest our data without our authorization. Take back your privacy with #Sentinel

Privacy

Sentinel is built from the ground up to value your privacy above all else. When using Sentinel you are anonnymous, all traffic to and from Sentinel is encrypted and travels through the tor network. All software included with sentinel is opensourse and throughroughly checked to insure no data leakage can occur.

Security

Sentinel is one of the most secure operating systems out of the box. This is possible becuase sentinel is preconfigured to be as secure as possible. Core steps are taken to insure this, such as; Closeing all unused ports, Strict firewall rules, systemwide application sandboxing and our Sentinel Anti-Virus.

Open-Sourse Code

You don't have to trust us, you can check out the code for yourself. We believe to be truly private and secure, the code should be open-sourse and the community should be able to crititisie and test for issues and vunerabilities. That way, you don't have to take our word for it.
